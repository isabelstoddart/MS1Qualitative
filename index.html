Carl Newman's
Faceless Nudes
Carl Newman was an American artist from Pennsylvania famous for his collection of nude figures. The Smithsonian houses a collection of 258 of these drawings and about 50% of them are faceless. Why did Carl Newman draw so many faceless nudes? Is it because faces are hard to draw? Is it for the animosity of the model? Is it because naked bodies are more appealing than faces? Explore the visualization and decide for yourself!
